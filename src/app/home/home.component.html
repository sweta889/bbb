What is Business Buddies?

Business Buddies (BB) is a networking forum where likeminded individuals can promote and enhance their respective businesses by collaborating (with) and supporting other businesses. Following the motto of “Together, we grow,” BB consists of over 100 member, and is steadily growing with individuals from ALL business backgrounds joining the clan.

Based out of Vadodara, and soon to expand in other metropolitan cities, BB is a great platform to find new connections that can help a wide range of businesses generate more revenue in the long run.

About us

We like to call Business Buddies a binding community where people come together to “get business and give business.” Through social media pages, our WhatsApp group, interactive live events, and help desk line, BB members have access to details of others' ventures that helps generate possible link ups, collaborations, tie-ups, and authentic alliances.

Our team of five founding members, who themselves are business owners, founded BB in 2018 with a goal of fulfilling requirements of various businesses around the city of Vadodara. Their initial motive was to bring business owners together so that they can help each other thrive. BB started with 40 members and now proudly houses over 100 people in the community.

The entire structure of Business Buddies relies on five foundations of being non-judgemental, sharing fruitful experiences, learning, family bonding, and travelling. Read the detailed description of our foundations below :

Our community does not tolerate any kind of judgement regarding anyone's background or ventures. Every business, no matter the size or industry, won't be evaluated or judged by anyone over at BB.

Sharing experiences is a massive part of any growing community. We expect our members to share their positive and negative experiences with other members to help each other. However, these stories have to strictly be experiences than a business owner has experienced themselves; they shouldn't be advices or unwanted knowledge.

Learning is a forever process and BB proudly hosts in-person events where we tie up with certain organisations and individuals who can help us learn new things.

We believe in families. Therefore, BB members' spouses are also welcome to join and participate in our events. We understand that many businesses run within a family where a number of people might be involved. So, everyone's welcome. The objective of this practice is to break an enigma of bias. However, we do expect our leading members to not overuse this foundation.

BB hosts a wide range of activities that involve travelling and excursions. We believe that there's no better way to bond than travel together. We'd love it if our members can make time for such events and join us on these memorable events.

Activities

Keeping our foundations in mind, Business Buddies hosts a wide range of in-person activities and events.

All the Business Buddies members are invited to two in-person meet-ups every month. We believe that face-to-face conversations are always better than online ones, therefore, we host a sesh where new members can meet the old members, share experiences, find new collaborators, or simply to promote their businesses.

Every now and then, BB founders strive to organise “learning” based events such as symposiums, lectures, debates and workshops where BB members can learn new things. The invites will be sent out via social media so the members can choose whether or not an event is of use to them or not.

If a BB member feels as if they can benefit from another business, our team is happy to set up one-on-one meets to discuss the details on how two businesses can collaborate. And do not worry, we don’t expect any commission to do this.
