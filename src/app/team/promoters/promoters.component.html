Proprietor of Hexen Engineering, an Industrialist into Manufacturing and Export of High Precision Castings to many leading countries. He supplies goods to many industries like Automotive, Railway, Power, Steel, Refrigeration, Marine, Pump & Valve etc. He started Hexen back in 1993 all by himself and brought it to where it is today. He is also among one of the top Art Collectors of Gujarat. He has an Art Collection of thousands of paintings made by leading artists for India ranging from works of M.S Hussian, Ramkumar, Jeram Patel etc. He is also involved in specialized work of Cleaning and Coating of Monuments and Temples. He is socially very well connected with NGOs, trekking clubs and charities.
